💡 Habit Building Tips

  • Start small - choose habits that take less than 5 minutes
  • Be consistent - do your habit at the same time every day
  • Track progress - seeing your streaks will motivate you
  • Don't break the chain - even one day off resets your streak
  • Celebrate wins - reward yourself for reaching milestones
  • Be patient - it takes 21+ days to form a new habit
